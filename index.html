Meet the Doctor
Opened 1979.
Four dentists today.

"Dr. Goettee opened the doors in 1979. The practice that outlasts the dentist is the practice that does it right."

West Cobb Dentistry began in 1979 when Dr. David L. Goettee, DDS — Emory University School of Medicine 1977 — opened the practice in Smyrna. Forty-six years later he still sees patients on a limited basis while a four-dentist team carries the standard forward. graduated from Marquette University School of Dentistry in and has been practicing in Kennesaw ever since. AGD Fellow, ADA member, Invisalign-trained.

Today's lead: Dr. Tia Harding, DDS (Marquette University School of Dentistry, relocated to ATL 2007). Plus Dr. Dipa Thakkar, DDS (University of Michigan 2009, restorative + implants, Golden Toothbrush Award 2019) and Dr. Mark Dagher. Six experienced hygienists. 100+ verified 5-star reviews.
